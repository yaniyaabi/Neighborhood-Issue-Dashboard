About the Project

Visualizing NYC Neighborhood Issues with Data

We built an interactive dashboard that helps New Yorkers and policymakers better understand neighborhood-level challenges across the city. Our project highlights disparities in housing, sanitation, noise, and more—making it easier to act on the issues that matter most.

  • Four key issue categories across two neighborhoods.
  • Built on official NYC datasets to support informed decisions.
  • Designed for both citizens and city agencies.
